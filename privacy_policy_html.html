Privacy Policy for Simple Water Widget

Last Updated: October 10, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile application "Simple Water Widget" (hereinafter referred to as "App"), provided by Frederik Keimeier (hereinafter referred to as "we", "us", "our").

We take the protection of your personal data very seriously and handle your personal data confidentially and in accordance with legal data protection regulations and this Privacy Policy.

2. Controller

Frederik Keimeier
Email: simplewaterwidget@gmail.com

3. What Data is Collected?

3.1 Locally Stored Data

The App stores the following data exclusively locally on your device:

  • Water intake entries: Date, time, and amount of added beverages
  • Daily goals: Your personally set drinking goals
  • Reminder settings: Times and intervals for notifications
  • App settings: Language, theme, widget configuration
  • Statistics: Calculated weekly and monthly statistics based on your entries

Important: All this data is stored ONLY on your device and is NOT transmitted to us or third parties.

3.2 Data NOT Collected

We do NOT collect, store, or process any of the following data:

  • Personal identification data (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos or media files
  • Device information
  • Usage statistics or analytics

4. Permissions

The App requires the following Android permissions:

  • Notifications (POST_NOTIFICATIONS): To remind you to drink water
  • Run at startup (RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED): To restore reminders after a device restart
  • Exact alarms (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM): For precise reminders

All these permissions serve exclusively the functionality of the App and are not used for any other purposes.

5. Data Sharing

Your data is NOT shared, sold, or transmitted to third parties. Since all data is stored exclusively locally on your device, we have no access to your information.

6. Data Security

The App stores all data in a local, encrypted database on your device. The security of your data depends on your device's security settings.

7. Your Rights

Since we do not collect or process personal data, you have full control over your data:

  • Delete: You can delete all data at any time in the App settings
  • Export: Your data remains on your device and is automatically deleted upon uninstallation
  • Control: You have full control over all information stored in the App at all times

8. Data Upon Uninstallation

When you uninstall the App, all locally stored data is automatically deleted by the system. No data remains on servers or in the cloud, as the App does not use cloud services.

9. External Links

The App contains no links to external websites or third-party services.

10. Third-Party Services

The App does NOT use third-party services such as:

  • Analytics tools (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase)
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage
  • Social media integration

11. Children

The App is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect personal data, there are no special age restrictions.

12. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the App or legal requirements. Changes will be communicated via an App update.
